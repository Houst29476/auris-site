Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026 · Applies to Auris AI v1.0 on iOS and Android

The short version

Your audio recordings and transcripts stay on your device. Auris has no servers for your meeting data. Transcription, summarization, action-item extraction, and follow-up email drafting all happen locally on your phone via on-device AI models. We do not collect, sell, share, or fingerprint your meeting content. We have nothing to lose, leak, or hand over.

The one exception is your subscription status, which is processed by Apple or Google and relayed through RevenueCat strictly so the app knows whether you have Pro features unlocked. Details below.

On-device AI

Auris uses two on-device AI models:

  • Whisper (OpenAI's open-source speech recognition model) for transcription. Runs locally via the whisper.rn library on your phone's CPU/GPU. Audio is processed in memory and never transmitted.
  • Llama (Meta's open-source language model) for summarization, action item extraction, and follow-up email generation. Runs locally via the llama.rn library. The model file is downloaded once on first launch and stored in your app's sandbox.

Neither model communicates with a server during inference. Your meeting content is never sent to OpenAI, Meta, or any other party. We can verify this by inspecting the network behavior of the app — and so can you, with any network monitoring tool.

Third-party services

Auris uses one third-party service for billing:

  • RevenueCat — handles subscription receipts and entitlement validation. Receives only the anonymous identifier and subscription tier. RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

Auris does not use any analytics, ad networks, A/B testing tools, crash reporters, or marketing attribution SDKs in v1.0.

Your rights

  • Export everything. Settings → Export gives you a complete copy of your meeting summaries, action items, and client list in a portable format. The export never touches our servers.
  • Delete everything. Settings → Privacy → "Wipe Auris" removes every meeting, transcript, action item, and client from your device. Because there is no server-side copy, there is nothing else to delete.
  • Cancel your subscription. Through your App Store or Google Play subscription settings, at any time, without contacting us. The app continues to function in free mode after cancellation.

Recording laws

Recording conversations is regulated differently around the world. Some jurisdictions require all parties to consent to being recorded; others require only one party. You are responsible for complying with the recording laws in your jurisdiction and for obtaining any required consent from meeting participants. Auris provides the tools; you control how and when to use them.

Children

Auris is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we ever add a feature that changes what data is collected — for example, turning on analytics or cloud-based transcription — we will update this page, update the App Store and Google Play privacy disclosures, and update the in-app Privacy screen in the same release. The most recent revision date is shown at the top of this page.
